Missing Audit Logging for Sensitive Actions
Found by Milarna — Missing Data Analyst
critical C10 M

Detail

Only REQ056-REQ057 mention 'security' in vague terms (lockout, encryption). No mention of audit logging anywhere in 153 requirements. Compliance standards require immutable logs of all privileged actions.

Impact

No accountability trail for fraudulent transactions; refund abuse undetected; admin malice impossible to track; compliance failures (SOC 2, PCI-DSS require audit logs)

Recommendation

Add NFR: 'The system SHALL maintain immutable audit logs for: (1) All customer account changes (address, phone, email), (2) Payment transactions and refunds, (3) Admin discounts/overrides applied to orders, (4) Inventory adjustments > threshold, (5) Admin login/logout. Logs SHALL record timestamp, user ID, action, old value, new value, IP address, and be retained per data retention policy. Audit logs SHALL be read-only after creation.'
Missing Security Feature: Payment Card Data PCI Compliance
Found by Milarna — Missing Data Analyst
critical C10 M

Detail

REQ090 validates card details but assumes cards are entered directly in nopCommerce form. This is PCI non-compliant. Must use payment gateway APIs or hosted payment pages.

Impact

Direct card storage = PCI-DSS violation; massive compliance penalties ($5K-$100K per incident); data breach liability catastrophic; merchant account termination

Recommendation

Add requirement: 'The system SHALL NOT store raw credit card data. Payment processing SHALL use one of: (1) Payment gateway API with tokenization (cards stored with payment processor), (2) Hosted payment page (payment processor hosts form, nopCommerce receives token only), (3) 3D Secure redirect. The system SHALL encrypt all payment-related data in transit (TLS 1.2+). PCI-DSS compliance SHALL be documented: (a) System deemed 'SAQ A' (outsource all card handling to gateway), OR (b) achieve annual PCI-DSS certification from qualified security assessor. Store shall provide proof of PCI compliance to customers upon request.'

Unpredictable Outcome: Race Condition in Stock Validation at Order Confirmation
Found by Paul — Prediction Analyst
critical C9 P

Detail

REQ030, REQ040, and REQ098 specify stock validation at cart add and confirmation, but requirements are silent on: (1) whether stock is RESERVED during checkout or only validated at confirmation, (2) the exact behavior when validation fails at confirmation (block order? auto-reduce quantity? split into partial/backorder?), (3) whether a customer lock/reservation mechanism exists to prevent race conditions. System processes the order and then notifies the customer post-hoc that partial fulfillment will occur (backorder for insufficient units)

System allows overselling by 200% and promises delivery in 3 months without notifying the customer
