CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE
================================================================================

To customize these templates for your project:

1. Update Project Context
   - Replace nopCommerce references with your project name
   - Update feature lists with your specific features
   - Modify team member names and assignments
   - Update timelines and budgets

2. Customize Sample Data
   - Replace example test cases with actual test cases
   - Update risk descriptions with project-specific risks
   - Modify quality characteristics as needed
   - Add/remove items to match your scope

3. Adjust Formatting
   - Change color scheme if needed (header fill, alternate rows)
   - Modify column widths for your content
   - Adjust font sizes or styles
   - Add company branding

4. Scale for Your Team
   - Adjust number of test tasks based on team size
   - Modify story points based on your velocity
   - Update assignments to match your team structure
   - Add rows for additional features or risks
